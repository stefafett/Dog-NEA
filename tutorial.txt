Welcome to the tutorial.
To win the game you must do better in one these categories:
Intelligence, this is measured between 1 and 100, the highest score wins.
Exercise, this is measured between 1 and 10, the highest score wins.
Friendliness, this is measured between 1 and 10, the highest score wins.
Drool, this is measured between 1 and 10, the lowest score wins the round.

This is a round based game. The player starts by picking a category. If the player wins they pick again.
If the computer wins the last round the computer picks the category randomly.

Good luck player and have fun!
